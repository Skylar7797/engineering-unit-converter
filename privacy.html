Privacy Policy

01. Browser-Side Sovereignty

EUC.com operates on a Zero-Data-Transfer principle. Unlike traditional converters that send your data to a server for processing, our tools utilize JavaScript to perform all calculations locally within your browser. This architectural choice ensures that sensitive engineering data or security tokens never leave your machine.

02. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not use databases to store your inputs, nor do we implement server-side logging for your queries. Your MD5 hashes, JWT payloads, and unit conversion values exist only in your device's volatile memory (RAM) while the tab is active.
