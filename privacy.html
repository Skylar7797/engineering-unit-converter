Privacy Policy

Engineering Workbench is fully committed to protecting user privacy. This platform does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users. Users can freely perform calculations, unit conversions, and other engineering tasks without the need to provide personal identifiers, email addresses, or any form of account registration. All data entered by users, including notes, calculation history, or temporary session data, exists only locally on the user’s device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party entities.

The platform operates entirely client-side for sensitive operations. This means that any notes, calculation results, or unit conversion activities remain within the user’s browser session unless the user explicitly exports or saves them manually. No analytics scripts, tracking pixels, or cookies are employed that could associate activity with individual users. By design, Engineering Workbench ensures that user interactions are completely anonymous and secure, providing a privacy-first environment for all engineering tasks.

Although no personal data is collected, minimal non-identifiable technical information such as browser type or screen resolution may be temporarily accessed by the platform strictly for compatibility purposes and to optimize layout rendering. This data is used solely to improve user experience and is not stored, shared, or analyzed in any way that could compromise anonymity. Users retain full control over their content and session data, and there is no mechanism on the platform that could associate activity with a user identity.

Engineering Workbench is committed to zero data retention. Upon closing the browser or refreshing the page, all session data is cleared automatically, ensuring that no residual information remains accessible. Users are encouraged to save any important notes or results manually if needed. In addition, the platform avoids all third-party advertising networks, trackers, or external plugins that could compromise privacy. Any embedded content or resources are strictly limited to essential scripts for functionality, without the purpose of user tracking.

By using Engineering Workbench, users can confidently perform calculations and engineering workflows with the assurance that their data is fully private and remains under their control. We maintain a strong commitment to privacy and transparency, and this policy may be updated occasionally to reflect enhancements in security practices or platform improvements. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policy periodically to stay informed, though no changes will affect the core principle: Engineering Workbench never collects or shares personal information.

In summary, Engineering Workbench provides a secure, privacy-focused environment for engineers, students, and hobbyists alike. All operations are local, anonymous, and free from data collection. Users can rely on the platform for confidential work without concern for personal data exposure, tracking, or unauthorized sharing. The platform’s commitment to privacy-first design ensures that all interactions are fully protected, making Engineering Workbench a trusted tool for all engineering computations.
