Privacy Policy

At Engineering Workbench, user privacy is taken very seriously. Any information provided by users, including notes, calculation history, and unit conversion data, is kept strictly confidential and is never shared with third parties. The platform operates under secure protocols to ensure that all interactions are encrypted and safe from unauthorized access.

We may collect basic technical information for performance analysis and user experience improvements, such as browser type, device, or session data. However, this information is anonymized and used solely to enhance functionality and reliability of the platform. No personal data is sold or distributed, and users retain full control over their own notes and history.

By using Engineering Workbench, users agree to this privacy policy. The platform may update this policy occasionally to reflect improvements or changes in technology, and users are encouraged to review it periodically. Our commitment is to provide a secure, reliable environment where calculations and engineering work can be performed with confidence.
