About Engineering Workbench

Engineering Workbench is a practical, engineering-focused tool designed for environments where calculations and unit conversions are part of everyday work. By integrating a unit converter and a scientific calculator into a single interface, it reduces the friction of switching between multiple tools and references during problem-solving.

The unit converter is organized around physical quantities, allowing quick access to commonly used categories such as length, mass, energy, and pressure—parameters frequently encountered in analysis, design, and manufacturing contexts. Results are presented clearly and without unnecessary visual clutter.

The accompanying calculator focuses on essential engineering functions, including trigonometric operations, logarithms, and exponential expressions. Features such as calculation history and angle mode switching help maintain workflow continuity.

Rather than serving only as a learning aid, Engineering Workbench is being refined with the intention of becoming a dependable workspace—one that quietly supports accurate thinking and efficient problem-solving over time.
